This Privacy Policy describes how Owe (“the App”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use our iOS app. Owe is published by the developer of the App (contact below). We designed Owe so you can calculate and track debt without connecting a bank account.

Summary

  • We do not sell your data or use third-party advertising analytics.
  • Loan and debt information you enter is stored on your device by default.
  • We do not require an account to use the calculators.
  • Optional Pro features may use Apple services (subscriptions, iCloud) and Kiara AI (described below).

Information you provide

You may enter financial information such as loan balances, interest rates, payment amounts, lender names, purchase details, and notes. This data is used only to power calculators, charts, reminders, and tracking features inside the App.

If you use the SMS import feature, you paste text yourself. The App parses that text on your device to suggest purchase fields. We do not automatically read your messages or SMS inbox.

If you use the document vault, photos or files you attach are stored locally on your device (and in your device backup if you back up to iCloud or a computer).

Information stored on your device

By default, your tracked loans, purchases, settings, compare scenarios (Pro), calculator history, Kiara chat messages during your session, and app preferences are stored locally using on-device storage (including Apple’s UserDefaults and local files). We do not operate our own servers for core app data.

Kiara AI assistant (Pro)

Kiara is an optional Pro feature that answers educational questions about loans and debt using artificial intelligence. When you use Kiara:

  • Text you send (and optional on-screen context such as which calculator you are viewing) is transmitted from your device to our Cloudflare Worker proxy to generate a reply.
  • Our proxy may forward that text to a third-party AI provider (for example OpenAI or Cloudflare Workers AI) to produce a response. We do not send your full loan database or bank details — only what you type or what the App includes as screen context for that chat.
  • Voice input: if you use the microphone, speech is converted to text on your device using Apple’s speech recognition. Audio is not sent to our servers.
  • Kiara does not perform loan math on the server; calculations stay on your device. Kiara explains concepts and on-screen results.
  • We do not use Kiara conversations for advertising. Third-party AI providers process requests under their own terms and policies. Do not send passwords, full account numbers, or other highly sensitive credentials in chat.

Optional iCloud partner sync (Pro)

If you enable Partner sync (iCloud), selected debt data is shared through Apple’s CloudKit service with a partner you invite. Data is transmitted and stored under your Apple ID and Apple’s iCloud terms. We do not receive a copy on our servers. You can disable sync at any time in Settings.

Apple In-App Purchase and subscriptions

Owe Pro is available as an auto-renewing monthly or annual subscription processed by Apple. Some users may have a grandfathered lifetime purchase from an earlier version; that status is also verified through Apple. We do not receive your payment card details. Apple may provide us with purchase or subscription status (for example, whether Pro is active) to enable features. Subscription management and cancellation are handled in your Apple ID settings.

Device permissions

With your permission, the App may access:

  • Notifications — local payment, promo, recap, and Kiara insight reminders you configure.
  • Calendar — to add payment events you choose to create.
  • Camera / Photo Library — to add document images to your vault.
  • Microphone — for Kiara voice questions (speech-to-text on device).
  • Speech recognition — to convert your voice to text for Kiara on device.
  • Face ID / Touch ID — to lock the App or document vault on your device.

You can revoke permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

Widgets

If you add an Owe home-screen widget, summary information you already stored in the App may be displayed on your device. Widget data stays within Apple’s widget system on your device.

Backup and export

If you use backup or export features, files are created on your device or saved to a location you choose (for example Files, email, or cloud storage you control). We do not receive those exports.

Support and feedback email

If you email us at ravicraj@zohomail.in or use in-app feedback, we receive whatever you choose to send (for example, your message, email address, and optional device/app version details). We use that information only to respond to your request.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
